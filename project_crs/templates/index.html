How it works

The software detects candlestick patterns and analyses market trends by collecting a month's worth of historical data.
Based on the last hour's data, it then presents your selected pattern and generates a trade proposal.
For real-time effectiveness of this pattern, users must manually execute this trade.

WHO WE ARE?

We are a site dedicated to empowering traders with cutting-edge financial analysis tools.
Our platform meticulously gathers a month's worth of historical market data to uncover meaningful candlestick patterns and trends.
By presenting these insights and generating precise trade proposals based on the latest hour of data, we equip our users with the knowledge necessary to make informed trading decisions.
To bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, we encourage our users to manually execute trades, enabling them to experience the real-time effectiveness of our analytical tools.
